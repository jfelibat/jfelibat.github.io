Jesús I. Feliciano-Batista

Jesús Feliciano-Batista is a young Puerto Rican journalist with a deep love for his craft. Coming from a family that values sports, Jesús developed a passion for telling stories related to this field, and the people who equally find passion in it. Moreover, Jesús is a sports journalist with a focus on some of the parts of sports that others in the field tend to forget. As proven by his piece on Oshae Brissett, Feliciano-Batista focuses on the human aspect of sports, more than just performances. Jesús' work has been featured in local Puerto Rican newspapers, online sports media outlets and SU campus publications.
